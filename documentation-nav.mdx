This documentation site is our knowledge base for all things ProjectDiscovery. We offer information about all of our solutions to support potential customers, new users, and our existing community.

We're continuously working to provide a centralized and comprehensive set of resources to help you explore our tools and democratize security for everyone.

<Info> Didn't find what you were looking for? Check out [GitHub](https://github.com/projectdiscovery) to explore other tools and content. </Info>

### Navigating the Docs

Here are a few tips on how to get around: 
- Use search to locate a specific term or topic. For more specific results, use a phrase or question for a detailed AI-powered response. 
- Navigate through sections with the tabs at the top (Tools, Templates, Cloud Platform, etc.) 
- Check out the [Help](/help) tab for support and info on joining our community

## Content Overview 

Let's dive into the sections of our docs site:

- [**Tools**](/tools): Delve into the suite of open source tools crafted by ProjectDiscovery. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just getting started, this section breaks down each tool's purpose, features, and how-tos.

- [**Nuclei Templates**](/templates): Learn all about Nuclei templates - powerful blueprints that drive precise vulnerability detection for both Nuclei and PDCP. Get insights on their structure, usage, and how to customize them for your unique requirements.

- [**Cloud Platform**](/cloud): **ProjectDiscovery Cloud Platform (PDCP)** is a cloud-hosted security platform to protect your attack surface at scale. Discover the capabilities, benefits, and step-by-step guides to harness its full potential.

- [**API**](/api-reference): Whether you're looking to integrate, automate, or expand, our cloud's API documentation has you covered. Dive into detailed endpoints, request-response patterns, and best practices to seamlessly weave ProjectDiscovery into your workflow.

- [**Help**](/help): Questions? In need of some guidance to create a specific workflow or resolve a particular issue? Check out help to review our support resources.  

Explore through these sections, and remember, knowledge empowers security. Happy exploring!
