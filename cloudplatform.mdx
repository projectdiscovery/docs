## What is ProjectDiscovery? 

A cloud-hosted security platform designed to provide continuous visibility across your external attack surface by detecting exploitable vulnerabilities and misconfigurations. 

<Tip>[Try our cloud platform for free](https://cloud.projectdiscovery.io/).</Tip>

It is built to solve a variety of use cases, and scale to support the key workflows application security teams need to secure their infrastructure.
